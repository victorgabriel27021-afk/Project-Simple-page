NIKE

Nike is one of the world's largest sporting goods brands. Founded in 1971, it became famous for its slogan "Just Do It" and the iconic Swoosh. The brand is closely linked to basketball and soccer, sponsoring top athletes and investing heavily in innovation and performance.

ADIDAS

Adidas is a German brand created in 1949. Recognized by its three stripes, it combines sport and urban style. It has a strong presence in world football and also in streetwear, uniting technology, comfort, and modern design.

PUMA

Puma was founded in 1948 and is also German. The brand stands out for its bold design and the connection between sport and fashion. It has a strong presence in football, running, and lifestyle, always offering models with a striking visual appeal.

New Balance

New Balance is known for the comfort and quality of its sneakers. Founded in 1906, the brand stands out for its performance-focused production as well as its casual style, making it very popular for everyday wear.
